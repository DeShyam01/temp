Money Heist

Release Date: May 2, 2017 Original Language: Spanish. Money Heist follows a mysterious mastermind known as The Professor who recruits eight criminals with unique skills to carry out an ambitious plan—to rob the Royal Mint of Spain and print billions of euros. Each member adopts a city name as a code and agrees to follow a strict set of rules. As hostages are taken and the police close in, complex emotions and strategies unfold in a high-stakes cat-and-mouse game.

Dark

Release Date: December 1, 2017 Original Language: German. Dark is a mind-bending German science fiction series set in the small town of Winden, where the disappearance of a child reveals a web of secrets spanning multiple generations. As four interconnected families uncover a time travel conspiracy that spans centuries, they confront questions of fate, free will, and the cyclical nature of time. The series intricately weaves together personal drama, philosophical themes, and paradoxes, making it one of the most complex and gripping shows of its kind.

Mai

Release Date: April 15, 2022 Original Language: Hindi. Mai: A Mother’s Rage is an Indian crime thriller that follows Sheel, a meek, middle-aged nurse whose daughter is tragically killed in a hit-and-run. As she digs deeper into the incident, Sheel discovers a dangerous underworld of crime and corruption. Her transformation from a grieving mother to a relentless seeker of justice unravels shocking truths and tests the limits of morality and maternal strength. Driven by love and loss, Sheel stops at nothing to uncover the truth.

Betaal

Action|Supernatural|1 Season|4 Episodes

Betaal

Release Date: May 24, 2020 Original Language: Hindi. Betaal is an Indian zombie horror series that follows a group of soldiers deployed to displace tribal villagers from a remote area to make way for a highway. Unknowingly, they open an ancient tunnel sealed for decades—releasing a cursed battalion of undead British soldiers led by the sinister Colonel Lynedoch. As the undead rise, the soldiers and villagers must fight for survival against supernatural forces, revealing dark secrets, colonial history, and moral dilemmas.
