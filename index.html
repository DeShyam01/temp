Betaal is an Indian zombie horror series that follows a group of soldiers deployed to displace tribal villagers from a remote area to make way for a highway. Unknowingly, they open an ancient tunnel sealed for decades—releasing a cursed battalion of undead British soldiers led by the sinister Colonel Lynedoch. As the undead rise, the soldiers and villagers must fight for survival against supernatural forces, revealing dark secrets, colonial history, and moral dilemmas.
